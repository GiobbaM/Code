About me

I was born on the 27th of July, in a small city in Italy, under the Alps and close to Switzerland.
When I was 20 I moved to Milan where I studied at Politecnico of Milan as a communication designer where I studied mainly graphic design and brand identity but, more important, there I learned also the design thinking, as a way to explore new solutions and possibility to reach the best output. After three years I got my degree and then I decided to start working and put in practice what I had learned.
I had already worked for an agency during my university years but at that time my skills and knowledge were too few, but after my degree I was ready to work in a team of professionals. I worked for a digital consulting agency called Digitag Consulting, as a graphic designer but my tasks often included some work as Social Media Manager Junior, at the end of the contract I decided to move to Denmark to study more as a web designer because it is what I like the most. Here I'm living in Aalborg and since September 2017 I'm studying at the UCN as Multimedia Designer.
