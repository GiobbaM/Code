Museum Exhibition
Vincent Frost

Museum Exhibition - Vincent Frost is an exercise that I did during my university years in Italy. The aim was to design a poster for the presention of a design exhibition in a museum, and the main artist was Vincent Frost (a famous graphic designer known especially for his typography works). The idea therefore was to create an output that could combine Frost's style with the museum visual identity.
