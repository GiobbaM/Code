World Traveler

World Traveler is one of my first websites, in this project I wanted to imagine how the website for an Hi-end travel agency could be. The idea was to find a few destinations and there simulate an hotel with its own facilities and caracteristics, but on the website the customers could find hotels but also 'experiences'. The website therefore should give a preview of what the agency offers.
