MyDay Fundraising
Campaign

MyDay Fundraising Campaign is part of a bigger job that I did for my degree in Italy, the goal of the association MyDay is to help families in need to organize a birthday party for their children, so this poster should help and create an empathy with the probable user, as well as spreading the message about what the association was doing. The design was done by some local children with their wishes for their birthday.
